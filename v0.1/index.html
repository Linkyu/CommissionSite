Commission info

Last update: January 21st, 2018

Hello, and welcome to my public art page! I am currently taking commissions, and this is where you can find all the info you might need about it. If you already know what you want and what price to expect for it, there is a form at the bottom of this page to contact me for the commission.

tl;dr: Base prices go from $10 to $150, with an average around $30 for colored portraits.

Base prices

Please note that these prices are for a single character. For scenes and extra characters, please see the modifiers further below.

Price modifiers

The prices above are for simply drawing one character, full-size. Anything more (or less, eg. portraits) will change the price accordingly.

Quick rules

What I do draw

Anything, really, including humans, animals, machines, and everything in between, SFW or not.
Yes, that includes furries and mechas.

What I don't draw

Extreme gore, illegal content, hate propaganda, shocking material, etc. You get the idea.

Payment

50% down payment, 50% at the completion prior to the product delivery.
